Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically, we can deliver a basic website within 7-10 working days after receiving all your content (text, images, etc.). More complex projects may take 2-3 weeks.

Do I need to pay for hosting?

Our pricing includes 1 year of basic hosting. After the first year, you can continue with our hosting service (₹999/year) or transfer to any hosting provider of your choice.

Can I update my website myself?

Yes! We provide simple instructions for basic updates. For more complex changes, we offer affordable maintenance packages.

What if I don't like the design?

We'll work with you until you're completely satisfied. You'll see the initial design before we proceed with development and can request changes at that stage.

Do you provide domain registration?

Yes, we can help you register a domain name (typically ₹599-999/year depending on the extension). Alternatively, you can register your own domain and we'll set it up for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, bank transfers, and cash (for local clients in Moradabad). A 50% advance is required to start the project, with the balance due upon completion.
